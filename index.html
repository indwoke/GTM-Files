II. Engineering & Tech Moat
Algorithmic cohort tracking resolves attrition bias by converting demographic churn into actionable systemic risk intelligence.

The Headcount Variable (N)

Field workers log cohort size (N) alongside the cryptographic hash during every assessment cycle (Baseline vs. Post-Assessment).

Detecting Variance (ΔN)

If cohort N drops significantly (e.g., from 15 to 9), the analytics engine automatically flags high migration or dropout rates.

Systemic Risk Output

This strict boundary prevents flawed RCT mean comparisons while delivering valuable macro-trend migration data directly to donors.
